Introduction

Hello, I'm Edward, a software developer based in London. For over twelve years I've been working professionally on full stack web application development based around the Microsoft .NET framework. Embracing new technologies, I'm driven by the desire to learn, and a passion for the subject. I have pixel-perfect attention to detail, and focus on developing very rich and interactive solutions whilst keeping them as lean and lightweight as possible. With a background in art and design, I really enjoy, and excel at front end development, and due to the increasing vastness of web application development as a subject, I'm looking to re-focus my career in this direction.

I have superb interpersonal and social skills which translate into being able to liaise effectively with technical and non-technical peers, and provide an excellent ability to turn user requirements, and business rules and use cases into successful software solutions.
